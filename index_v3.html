WHAT IS EOTC WEEK?

As part of the Year 10 programme students will be participating in four days of Education Outside the Classroom (EOTC). The focus of this programme is one of citizenship and social awareness.

BUT WHY SHOULD YOU DO IT?

Because Tawa College offers many fun activites!

The four days of activities in which your son/daughter will participate are as follows: Community Service, Rangituhi Walk, Adrenaline Forest/Te Rauparaha Arena Aquatic Centre and Wainuiomata Camp.

Activites

CAMP WAINUIOMATA

Surrounded by beautiful views, it’s the perfect spot to put up your tents, relax with friends, and become one with nature.

ADRENALIN FOREST + TE RAUPARAHA ARENA AQUATIC CENTER

Challenge your strength, endurance, and mindset at Adrenaline Forest. Then relax at the pool, where students can enjoy full access to all parts of the aquatic facility.

RANGITUHI WALK

Walk through some of Tawa most stunning native bush with breathtaking views!

COMMUNITY SERVICE

Community service is a meaningful part of our EOTC week, which offers students an opportunity to give back to the community that has supported them.
