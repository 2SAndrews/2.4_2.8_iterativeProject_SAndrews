WHAT IS EOTC WEEK?

As part of the Year 10 programme students will be participating in four days of Education Outside the Classroom (EOTC). The focus of this programme is one of citizenship and social awareness.

BUT WHY SHOULD YOU DO IT?

Because Tawa College offers many fun activites!

The four days of activities in which your son/daughter will participate are as follows: Community Service, Rangituhi Walk, Adrenaline Forest/Te Rauparaha Arena Aquatic Centre and Wainuiomata Camp.

Activites

CAMP WAINUIOMATA

Surrounded by beautiful terrain, it’s the perfect spot to pitch your tents, relax with friends, and immerse yourself in nature.

ADRENALINE FOREST + TE RAUPARAHA ARENA AQUATIC CENTER

Challenge your strength, endurance, and mindset at Adrenaline Forest. Then relax at the pool where students are welcome to enjoy full access to every part of the aquatic facility,

RANGITUHI WALK

The Rangituhi Walk is a breathtaking journey through some of New Zealand’s most stunning native bush.

COMMUNITY SERVICE

Community service is a meaningful part of our EOTC week, offering us a valuable opportunity to give back to the community that has supported us in so many ways.
