CONNECT WITH NATURE

RANGITUHI WALK

Elsdon, Porirua 5022

The Rangituhi Walk is a breathtaking tramp through some of Tawa’s beautiful native bush. In this scenic route winds its way through lush forest blooming with beautiful native flora. While the walk is around 6.5k and might feel boring or challenging at times, the company of good friends makes the journey fly by. Along the way, you'll be treated to stunning views perfect for a quick photo or just a quiet moment to take it all in. Whether you're a nature lover or just in it to spend time with your mates, Rangituhi Walk is an unforgettable experience!

LOOK AT THE VIEWS!

KEY INFORMATION

A private bus will drop students at the start of the Rangituhi walkway. Teachers will help students navigate the path to the lookout and then up to the summit of Rangituhi. From here students will traverse the tops and then pop down into Te Ngahere o Tawa. Students will spend more time on the walk learning about reading maps, trees and leaves, local places of interest from viewpoints etc. There is also a $50 prezzy card as a prize for activities students can complete on the way.
